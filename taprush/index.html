📋 Privacy Summary

TapRush respects your privacy. We don't collect, store, or share any personal information. All your game data stays on your device.

1. Information We Don't Collect

TapRush is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Crash reports or diagnostic data
  • Game statistics or scores
  • Any data that could identify you personally

🔒 Your Data Stays Local

All game data including your username, high scores, game statistics, and preferences are stored locally on your device using iOS's secure UserDefaults system. This data never leaves your device.

2. Data Storage

The only data TapRush stores is:

  • Username: The display name you choose for high scores (stored locally)
  • High Scores: Your best scores for each game mode (stored locally)
  • Game Settings: Your preferences and settings (stored locally)
  • Onboarding Status: Whether you've completed the initial setup (stored locally)

All of this information is stored exclusively on your iOS device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

3. Network Activity

TapRush operates completely offline and does not:

  • Connect to the internet
  • Send data to external servers
  • Download content from the web
  • Use any online services or APIs
  • Include advertising networks
  • Include analytics services

4. Third-Party Services

TapRush does not integrate with any third-party services, including:

  • Social media platforms
  • Advertising networks
  • Analytics services
  • Cloud storage services
  • Game centers or leaderboards

5. Children's Privacy

TapRush is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age. Since we don't collect any personal information from any users, this policy applies equally to users of all ages.

6. Data Security

Since all data remains on your device:

  • Your data is protected by iOS's built-in security features
  • Data is automatically backed up with your device backups (if enabled)
  • No data transmission means no risk of interception
  • Uninstalling the app will remove all stored data

7. Your Rights

Since we don't collect your data, there's no data for us to:

  • Access or view
  • Delete or modify
  • Share or sell
  • Transfer to third parties

You have complete control over your local data and can delete it anytime by uninstalling the app or resetting it within the app settings.

8. App Permissions

TapRush requests minimal permissions:

  • No camera access
  • No microphone access
  • No location access
  • No contacts access
  • No network access required

The app only uses standard iOS frameworks for user interface, local data storage, and haptic feedback.

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

If we make any changes to this privacy policy, we will:

  • Update this page with the new policy
  • Update the "Last Updated" date below
  • Include information about changes in app updates (if applicable)

We recommend checking this page periodically for any updates.

10. App Store Compliance

This privacy policy complies with:

  • Apple App Store Review Guidelines
  • iOS Privacy Requirements
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
