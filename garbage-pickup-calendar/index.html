Privacy Policy - Your Data Stays Private

🔒 Complete Privacy Protection

Simple Promise: Garbage Tracker never collects, stores, or shares any of your personal data. Everything stays on your device, always.

What Information We Collect

None. Garbage Tracker does not collect any personal information, usage data, analytics, or any other data. The app works entirely offline without requiring an internet connection.

How Your Data is Stored

  • Pickup Schedules: Stored locally using iOS secure storage
  • Custom Waste Types: Stored locally on your device only
  • Notification Settings: Stored locally on your device only
  • App Preferences: Stored locally on your device only

Important: If you delete the app, all data is permanently deleted. We cannot recover it because we never had access to it.

Notifications

The app requests permission to send local notifications for garbage pickup reminders. These notifications:

  • Are generated locally on your device
  • Do not transmit any data to external servers
  • Can be disabled anytime in iOS Settings
  • Contain only the pickup information you entered

Data Sharing

We don't share anything because we don't collect anything. Your garbage pickup schedule is completely private and remains only on your device.

Third-Party Services

Garbage Tracker does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or data collection services. No data is transmitted to any external services or companies.

Children's Privacy

Our app is completely safe for users of all ages as it does not collect any personal information whatsoever. No special parental controls are needed.

Data Security

Since all data remains on your device using iOS secure storage mechanisms, your information is protected by your device's built-in security features including device passcode, Touch ID, or Face ID.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect your data, there's nothing for us to delete, modify, or export. You have complete control over your data directly through the app interface.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update this page and notify users through app updates if necessary. The "Last updated" date will reflect any changes.
