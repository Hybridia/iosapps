Remindy

Smart task management for iPhone

A beautifully designed iPhone app for managing reminders, tasks, and notes with intelligent notifications and subtask management. Built with privacy in mind, all your data stays securely on your device.

Powerful Features

Everything you need to stay organized and productive, designed with privacy and simplicity in mind.

Smart Reminders

Create detailed reminders with notes, due dates, priority levels, and photo attachments. Rich text support for all your important details and context.

Subtask Management

Break down complex projects into manageable subtasks. Track progress with visual indicators and auto-complete features that keep you motivated.

Intelligent Notifications

Local notifications that respect your privacy. Flexible scheduling with preset options and comprehensive management tools for perfect timing.

Quick Creation

Fast reminder creation with one-tap time presets (1 Hour, This Evening, Tomorrow, Next Week) and instant priority setting for rapid task entry.

Smart Organization

Today, Tomorrow, and time-based sections keep you focused. Smart filtering by priority and completion status with personalized greetings.

Privacy First

All data stored locally on your device. No external servers, no analytics, no tracking. Full control and ownership of your data.
